Objective of Card Match

The game "Card Match" is a card guessing game in which the player is required to guess the cards in the deck. Card fans of all kinds will enjoy playing this game, but to win, a player must either be strategic or lucky.

Elements of the Game:
  • The game will display 52 buttons on the left side, each representing a playing card.
  • In the center of the page, there will be a grid that is initially hidden and will be revealed once the player clicks the correct card.
  • The player will be provided with counters for the total sum, score, and lives on the right side of the page.
  • Info button - explains the rules in case the player forgets the rules.
  • Reset button - to reset the grid and play the game again from the start
  • Exit button - this will direct the player to the start page
Additional Information:
  • The player needs to interact only with the deck of buttons to play the game.
  • Only a mouse is required to play this game.
  • This game is linear, meaning it does not allow the user to go back to the previous page.
Important information:
  • Once the player refresh or direct inbetween the pages to progress would not be saved. So avoid moving back and forth or refreshing the page during the game.
    • Game Rules:
    • Modes and Lives

      The game has three difficulty levels, differentiated by the count of lives. The number of lives is determined based on probability.

      • Easy mode has 18 lives.
      • Medium mode has 15 lives.
      • Hard mode has 12 lives.
    • Guessing and Scoring
      • If the player guesses the right card, the card will be revealed and the button color will change to green.
      • If the player guesses the wrong card, the card will not be revealed and the button color will change to red.
      • If the player guesses the right card value but the card suit is wrong, there will be no changes in the card, but the button color will change to yellow.
      • After guessing the rigth card, and the player clicks same card value but different suite still the life will be reduced because the cards values was not duplicated.
      • For every correct guess, the player will get 100 points.
      • For consecutive correct guesses, the player will get double the score (2 * 100).
      • For every wrong guess, including misguesses (indicated by a yellow button), one life will be deducted from the lives count.
    • Hints
      • No duplication of card value - The cards only exists once in the grid, the card value or card itself would not exist again in the grid.
      • Reveal cards - If the player loses the game, there will be a option to show the rest of the card in the deck, but this option would not be displayed if the player wins the game.
      • There are two counters displaying numbers which are the sums of the values of the cards.
      • Total Sum - The total sum value will change from game to game based on the values of the cards.
      • Remaining Sum - The remaining sum will change after every correct guess. The values will be reduced based on the card values which have been clicked by the user.
    • Card Values
      • Ace -> A = 1
      • The values from 2 to 10 have the same value as the card number
      • Jack -> J = 11
      • Queen -> Q = 12
      • King -> K = 13
